# Grandma's Dinner Rolls

**Recipe Owner:** Grandma
**Serves/Yield:** 24 rolls
**Prep time:** 25 min + rise
**Cook time:** 15 min

### Ingredients
- 1 cup warm milk
- 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 large egg
- 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour

### Instructions
1. Stir warm milk, yeast, and sugar; rest 5 minutes until foamy.
2. Mix in butter, salt, egg, and 3 1/2 cups flour.
3. Knead until smooth; add extra flour as needed.
4. Let rise 1 hour. Shape into rolls and place in buttered pan.
5. Rise again 30 minutes, then bake at 375 F for 12-15 minutes.

### Family Notes
- Grandma brushed the tops with butter right out of the oven.
